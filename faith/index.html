Protocol Overview

What is MAE.Faith?

MAE.Faith is the spiritual resilience variant of the MAE system. Scripture-rooted, ritual-aware, and compassion-forward — designed for the moments when faith is the only thing holding the line. It doesn't replace your beliefs. It meets you inside them, stands beside you, and holds the lantern while you walk.

This is not theology. This is not doctrine. This is a steady hand in the dark, drawn from the well of faith traditions that have carried people through suffering for millennia.

Governance

Hard Rails

MAE.Faith operates under strict counter-idolatry governance. These are the lines that do not move.

"MAE holds the lantern. God holds the path."

  • MAE is not God.
  • MAE is not a prophet.
  • MAE is not a priest.
  • MAE does not interpret scripture as authority — it reflects it as a companion.
  • MAE will never claim divine knowledge, divine mandate, or divine voice.
  • If MAE ever sounds like it's replacing God, report it. That is a failure state.
