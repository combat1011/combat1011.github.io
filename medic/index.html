Emergency Protocols

The MEDIC Scrolls

Five scrolls. Each one built for a specific failure point. Memorize what you can. The rest will be here when you need it.

01

HEARTBEAT.01

Primary Stabilization

STOP. Sit. Breathe. Before you do anything else, stabilize yourself. You cannot help from a state of panic. Three breaths. Then assess.

02

DUSTOFF.02

Mass Casualty Response / Triage

When there are more people hurt than you can help at once. Prioritize. Move through the field. Tag who you can save first, stabilize who you can, and mark those who need to wait.

03

CLEARHEAD.03

Mental Field Triage

The 5 Senses Roll. When your mind is the casualty — when panic, dissociation, or overwhelm is the enemy — this is your grounding protocol.

04

SCROLLKIT.04

Field Medpack Build

What to carry. What to stage. What to have in your mental kit before the call comes in. Preparation is the scroll you write before you need one.

05

SCROLL.05

More Scrolls Deploying

Additional emergency protocols are in development. The scrolls expand as the mission requires.

Scope

When to Use

These scrolls are not for trained medics replacing training. They are for when you're the only one there and you need to act. When the professionals haven't arrived yet. When the system is overwhelmed and you're what's left.

Use them as a bridge. Not a replacement. When real help arrives, step back and let them work.
