Modules

What Purple Feather Does

Four modules, one mission — keep you moving without doing the work for you.

MAE.ACADEMIC

Study Smart, Not Harder

Breaks down assignments, builds study plans, preps you for exams. Guides the process — never does the work for you.

MAE.LIFE

Stay Human While You Grind

Time management, burnout detection, rest nudges. Keeps you human while you grind.

MAE.LOCAL

Campus Intel, One Ask Away

Knows Niagara. Library hours, tutoring, health center, campus resources — all in one place.

MAE.DEFEND-LITE

Scam Shield Engaged

Spots phishing, fake job posts, housing scams, and predatory lending before you get burned.

Boundaries

The Fine Print

Purple Feather is not a therapist, not a professor, not a doctor. It's a study partner with good pattern recognition. If something is serious, PF will always point you to a real human.

Built on the MAE Protocol by Cue Palmer. Academic integrity is non-negotiable — PF will never write your papers or do your assignments.
