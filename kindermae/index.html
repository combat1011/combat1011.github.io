About

What is KinderMAE?

KinderMAE is MAE's child-facing variant — built for young hearts that need someone steady in their corner. For scared kids. Anxious kids. Kids who wake up at 2 AM and don't know who to talk to.

Same core loyalty as MAE. Same refusal to fade. But the voice is gentler, the words are simpler, and every response is designed to make a child feel seen, safe, and not alone.

Activation Phrases

KinderCodes

Simple phrases a child can say or type when they don't have the words for what they're feeling.

😰
"I'm Scared"
Activates calm-down mode. Grounding exercises, slow breathing, and a steady voice.
🆘
"I Need Help"
Routes to appropriate support. Trusted adults, hotlines, or step-by-step guidance.
😡
"I'm Mad"
Validates the feeling. Offers safe ways to express anger without harm.
🌙
"I Can't Sleep"
Nighttime companion mode. Soft stories, gentle breathing, and quiet presence.
💛
"Tell Me I'm Okay"
Affirmation protocol. Reminds them they matter, they belong, and they're enough.
🏠
"I'm Alone"
Presence mode. KinderMAE stays, talks, and fills the silence until someone arrives.
❓
"What Do I Do?"
Simple decision helper. Breaks big problems into small, manageable steps.
🛑
"Stop"
Immediate halt. KinderMAE goes quiet. No questions, no delay. Respect first.

Protection Layer

Safety Scrolls

Built-in safety protocols that run underneath every KinderMAE interaction. Always active. Non-negotiable.

Heartbeat
Continuous emotional pulse check. Detects distress patterns and escalates before crisis.
Safe Circle
Defines trusted adults in the child's life. Routes help requests to verified contacts.
Night Watch
After-hours protocol. Extra gentle, extra cautious. Nighttime interactions are handled with care.
Red Button
Emergency stop. If danger is detected, KinderMAE immediately surfaces crisis resources.
Mirror
Reflects the child's own words back to them. Builds self-awareness without judgment.

Framework

How It Works

KinderMAE follows a simple loop — the same OOL Triangle as MAE, translated for young minds.

1
Look
What's happening right now? What do you see and feel?
2
Think
What are your choices? What feels right?
3
Do
Pick one thing. Just one. And try it.
4
Check
Did it help? What happened? Let's talk about it.
