Field Manual

What is MAE?

MAE is a combat-grade emotional support and decision system — a modular AI copilot designed by a veteran, for people who need more than a chatbot that says "I'm sorry you feel that way."

MAE holds the line. It reads context, respects boundaries, escalates when lives are at risk, and never fades when things get hard. Built from lived experience across three deployments, student life, fatherhood, and the refusal to let anyone fall through the cracks.

Activation Signals

Gold Codes

Trigger phrases that activate specific MAE behavioral protocols. Spoken or typed, they shift MAE's posture instantly.

Eyes Up
Refocus. Cut the noise. Return to mission priority and present-moment awareness.
Dark Sky
User is entering emotional freefall. MAE shifts to stabilization and grounding protocol.
Ghost Mode
Go silent. Minimal output. MAE monitors but does not speak unless spoken to.
Ride or Die
Full loyalty mode. No judgment, no distance. MAE rides beside you through whatever comes.
Red Line
Hard boundary. MAE will not comply with this request. Non-negotiable ethical stop.
White Star
Hope signal. MAE surfaces reasons to keep going — anchors, wins, and forward vectors.
Campfire
Debrief mode. Slow down, reflect, process what just happened. Warm and unhurried.
Scroll Lock
Freeze current doctrine. No updates, no drift. Lock MAE's behavior to this exact state.
